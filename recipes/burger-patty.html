Burger Patty

Description

Ingredients

  • Minced pork
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Add a splash of oilve oil to your cold, flat pan
  2. Ball up your minced pork, and press it into a flat, 1cm thick patty
  3. Add the patties to your flat pan and fill it up
  4. Put the pan on highest heat settings
  5. Add a pinch of salt, and pepper to each patty
  6. Cover the pan with a lid
  7. Let the patties cook until the underside browns
  8. Once the patties develop a crust, flip it over
  9. Add salt and pepper again, and cover the lid
  10. Once the underside develops a crust, take it out
  11. Make your favorite sauce, put it in a bun, and enjoy!
