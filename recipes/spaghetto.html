Spaghetto

Description

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water, and add 3 teaspoons of salt
  2. Add your spaghetti into the pot, stirring constantly, and follow package instructions
  3. Heat the pan to medium heat, add a splash of olive oil
  4. Add minced garlic, and fry until it starts to slightly turn brown
  5. Add chopped tomatoes
  6. Add a ladel of spaghetti water into the pan
  7. Once almost cooked, lightly crush the tomatoes
  8. Once al dente, add spaghetti into the pan
  9. Add a few more ladels of spaghetti water, and mix thoroughly
  10. Add some torn basil leaves, mix well
  11. Plate your spaghetti, and finish off with a splash of olive oil
  12. Pour yourself a nice glass of red wine
